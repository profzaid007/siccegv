1.Penetration Testing

With the ubiquitous implementation of web applications to manage various economic infrastructures, the necessity to fortify them against both internal and external threats has become increasingly alarming in the virtual landscape.

I have a reasonable amount of experience with performing penetration tests on mobile and web applications due to my employment in a former Cybersecurity firm. I also posses an acute conceptual understanding of how various web applications' vulnerabilities manifest themselves and thus will be able to rely on my own abilities to discover flaws instead of solely relying automated tools.

I will perform a thorough automated and manual analysis of your web application with a comprehensive report compiled at the end which would consist of all the vulnerabilities discovered in your site and what mitigations can be used to both, protect your application against these flaws and decrease the prospects of similar flaws emerging in the future.

2.Linux System Administration

Any issues pertaining to Linux System Administration will be diagnosed and rectified.
